Red-Black Trees

Introduction

Red-Black Trees are a type of self-balancing binary search tree.

Properties

  • Nodes are colored either red or black.
  • The root is always black.
  • Every leaf (NIL) is inserted as red

Operations

  1. Insertion
  2. Deletion
  3. Search
